## 📊 Project Overview
Why do employees leave?  

This project uses **Tableau** to analyze employee attrition and uncover the key drivers behind workforce turnover.  
The dashboard transforms raw HR data into **actionable insights** that can support retention strategies.

---

## 🚨 Key Insights
- Employees with **low job satisfaction (Level 1–2)** are most likely to leave  
- Former employees earn **~$4,787 vs $6,833** → **compensation gap**  
- **110 employees left without promotion** → lack of career growth  
- **Sales department is high-risk**, with **19 critical employees flagged**  
- **Single employees (25.5%)** show the highest attrition rate  

---

## 📈 Critical Pattern Discovered
Attrition peaks at:
- **0 years since last promotion**
- **7 years since last promotion**

➡️ Employees leave when they feel:
- **Stuck (no growth)**  
- **Disappointed after promotion (unmet expectations)**  

---

## 🧠 Business Impact
This dashboard helps organizations:
- Identify **high-risk employee segments**
- Improve **retention strategies**
